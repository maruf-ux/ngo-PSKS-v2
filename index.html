About us

Pollibashi Samaz Kalyan Sangstha (PSKS) is a dedicated non-governmental organization (NGO) committed to the socio-economic development of neglected and disadvantaged communities, with a vision to establish a poverty-free society. Our mission revolves around creating opportunities for marginalized individuals, sustainable asset management, and skill enhancement to uplift the quality of life in society.
